Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 14, 2026

NailStyle Studio helps users create AI-generated nail preview images from a hand photo and a design description. This Privacy Policy explains how information is handled when you use the app.

Information You Provide

When you generate a nail preview, the app may send your selected hand photo, design description, and selected style options to our backend service in order to create the AI preview.

Photos and Camera Permissions

The app requests access to your camera or photo library only when you choose to take or select a hand photo. The app may also request permission to save generated previews to your photo library.

AI Backend Processing

Image generation is processed through a backend AI service. Your uploaded photo, design description, and style options are used to create the requested nail preview. We do not ask users to provide their own AI API keys.

Generated Images and Local Storage

Generated previews saved in the app gallery are stored locally on your device. The app may use local device storage, including UserDefaults and local cache files, to keep saved preview metadata and images.

Saving and Sharing

If you choose to save or share a generated preview, the action is handled by iOS system features such as Photos or the share sheet.

Children

NailStyle Studio is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

Contact and Support

For privacy questions, support requests, or data-related inquiries, contact us at: support@nailstylestudio.com
